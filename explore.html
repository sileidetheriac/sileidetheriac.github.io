Brazil is the largest country in South America. It is filled with natural beauty, such as the Amazon rainforest.

Brazilians are always happy and love to party. Carnival is celebrated every February, where everyone goes out on the streets to dance and watch parades.

Brazilian cuisine is filled with flavors and is highly influenced by European, African and Asian cuisines. Rice and beans are the main side dishes. Brazilians love meat, churrasco, pastries, cakes, cheese, and are known to be one of the friendliest people!
